About Us

Who We Are

CYDO — Climate and Youth Development Organization — is a youth-led non-governmental organization based in Bangladesh. Founded in 2020, CYDO mobilizes young people to take meaningful action on climate change, waste management, reproductive health, and inclusive education.

🔭

Vision

A sustainable, equitable Bangladesh led by empowered youth.

🎯

Mission

Mobilize youth for climate resilience, education, and social justice.

💎

Values

Integrity, inclusion, innovation, collaboration.

What We Do

Our Focus Areas

Four interconnected pillars driving sustainable development and youth empowerment across Bangladesh.

♻️

Waste Management

Promoting zero-waste communities through awareness campaigns, recycling drives, and sustainable waste disposal practices across urban and rural areas.

Learn More
🌟

Youth Empowerment

Building leadership skills, civic engagement, and entrepreneurial mindsets among young Bangladeshis through workshops, mentorship, and community-led initiatives.

Learn More
❤️

SRHR Awareness

Advocating for sexual and reproductive health rights through peer education, safe spaces, and evidence-based campaigns that break stigma and foster informed choices.

Learn More
📚

Quality Education

Ensuring access to quality, inclusive education for marginalized youth through digital literacy programs, scholarship campaigns, and teacher training initiatives.

Our Story

Meet the Founders

Three visionary young leaders who dared to imagine a climate-resilient Bangladesh and built CYDO from the ground up.

Co-Founder
KA
Kamrul Ahsan
Executive Director & Co-Founder

A climate activist and social entrepreneur with a background in environmental science. Kamrul has led grassroots campaigns across 15 districts of Bangladesh and represented youth voices at COP summits.

Co-Founder
PR
Priya Roy
Program Director & Co-Founder

A passionate SRHR advocate and women's rights champion. Priya established CYDO's flagship sexual health education programs and has trained over 300 peer educators across Bangladesh.

Co-Founder
MH
Minhaj Hossain
Strategy Lead & Co-Founder

An education reform advocate with expertise in inclusive curriculum design. Minhaj spearheaded CYDO's quality education pillar and partnership strategy, forging alliances with 15 national and international organizations.

Projects & Activities

Green Khulna: City-Wide Recycling Campaign
Mobilized 400 volunteers across 12 wards to collect and sort 8 tons of recyclable waste in partnership with Khulna City Corporation.

Education

Digital Literacy for Rural Youth
Launched 5 community learning centers across Bagerhat district, providing free digital skills training to 800 young people aged 14–25.
