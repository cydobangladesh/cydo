Who We Are

About CYDO

CYDO is a youth-led nonprofit organization dedicated to climate resilience, environmental sustainability, and empowering youth to become changemakers in their communities.

Building a Sustainable Future Together

Through community engagement, leadership development, and innovative environmental initiatives, CYDO works to create a greener, more resilient future led by youth.

Mission

Empower youth to lead sustainable climate solutions.

Vision

Create climate-resilient communities through youth action.

Values

Leadership, sustainability, equality, and collaboration.
